Temporary work-arounds with The Previewer

  • Naming folders/files: make sure to use an underscore(_) when naming your folders and files because the left navigation will not recognize the folders/files with any whitespaces.


  • Updating the form: Click the "Click to edit" link at the top of the page and the prepopulated form will open for you.
    NOTE: after you make your edits, you will need to Save and Close the form in order to preview the page properly (currently working on a solution, see point #7 below).


  • To preview your updates: At the top of the page, click the Refresh link . This way, you will not loose your navigation place on the left. If you refresh the browser, there is a bread crumb trail , for you to navigate back to your place.
  • Special Character Symbols library:
    NOTE: these are the only ASCII Character Codes that will work using the Previewer, if you would like to use others that are not on the list, please send an email using the feedback link below.
